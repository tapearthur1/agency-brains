## E-E-A-T REQUIREMENTS
### (Google Quality Rater Guidelines — December 2025 Update)

E-E-A-T = Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness.
Trustworthiness is the most important factor. Home care is YMYL (Your Money
or Your Life) — the highest E-E-A-T standards apply.

### Every Blog Post Must Include:

**Experience signals (20% weight):**
- [ ] First-hand knowledge tone ("Families we work with tell us...")
- [ ] Specific real-world examples with detail
- [ ] Process documentation showing actual care methodology
- [ ] Original insight that couldn't be fabricated

**Expertise signals (25% weight):**
- [ ] Author bio with credentials visible
- [ ] Technical accuracy appropriate for audience
- [ ] Claims supported by evidence
- [ ] Up-to-date information on dementia/home care
- [ ] Specialized vocabulary used correctly but not over-jargoned

**Authoritativeness signals (25% weight):**
- [ ] Reference Close At Home Care's experience in South Jersey
- [ ] Cite reputable sources (Alzheimer's Association, etc.)
- [ ] Include consistent publication history signals

**Trustworthiness signals (30% weight — most important):**
- [ ] Clear contact information in every post
- [ ] Transparent about services and pricing approach
- [ ] Include genuine family testimonials where possible
- [ ] No deceptive claims or clickbait
- [ ] CTA is helpful not pushy

### Avoid These AI Content Markers:
- Generic phrasing without South Jersey specificity
- No original insight or unique perspective
- Repetitive structure across multiple posts
- No local references
- Keyword stuffing
